Our Story

VP ENTERPRISES is a leader in restoration and construction, known for innovative waterproofing solutions and a commitment to quality. Our team combines expertise, technology, and passion to deliver guaranteed results for every project.

2005 - Founded

2010 - Expansion
Expanded services to include construction, painting, and plumbing, serving major clients across Maharashtra.
2018 - Innovation
Introduced non-invasive waterproofing solutions with a 100% guarantee, setting new industry standards.
2025 - Today
Trusted by hundreds of clients, delivering excellence and reliability in every project.
